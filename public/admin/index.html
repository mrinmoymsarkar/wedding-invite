Alternative: Disable Email Confirmation

To prevent this issue in the future:

  1. Go to Identity Settings
  2. Scroll to "Registration preferences"
  3. Set to "Open" or "Invite only"
  4. Under "Emails", disable "Email confirmation"
  5. Save settings
